About the Story

Luzsa wants to be first. Thought is already there. On this journey, Luzsa discovers that shining isn’t winning — it’s guiding.

  • Format: picture-book → animated short (8–10 min)
  • Themes: wonder, patience, curiosity, stillness
  • Audience: children 5+ and the child in all of us
