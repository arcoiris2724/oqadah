Our Mission

We seed curiosity about what light and thought share in common — through free, sovereign storytelling.

Why We Exist

To invite wonder without walls. We publish public-access shorts, guides, and archives that respect privacy and attention.

  • Public, free short films (no trackers, no paywalls).
  • Study guides for families, schools, and circles.
  • Open formats and sustainable files for longevity.

Principles

  • Clarity over noise.
  • Kindness as a design constraint.
  • Child-friendly, adult-deep language.
  • Sovereign data & simple tech.
