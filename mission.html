Our Mission

We weave thought and light into sovereign stories for children—and the child within every seeker of wonder.

Why We Exist

To seed curiosity about what light and thought share in common—through freely accessible animated parables, ethical publishing, and privacy-first technology.

  • Public, free short films that invite wonder (no trackers, no paywalls).
  • Study & discussion guides for families and classrooms.
  • Sovereign archives: your data and ours remain under user control.

Principles

  • Clarity over noise.
  • Kindness as design constraint.
  • Children-first language, adult-grade depth.
  • Open formats, sustainable files.
