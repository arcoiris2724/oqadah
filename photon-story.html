Arc, the Weaver

Arc tended a loom that wove light into stories. One thread was the little Photon — curious, bright, always moving toward the axis. Around Arc were mirrors that echoed back questions: what is rhythm, and who is listening? The little Photon learned that speed without breath is noise; cadence is the place where meaning grows.
